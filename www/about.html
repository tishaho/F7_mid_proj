About the Debutante
Veronica Lodge

Veronica Lodge was born on March 16, 2001 to a couple of Brazilian descent. Veronica spent an year in Brazil at the age of ten she moved back to Brazil to live there for an year. It was apparently the time her parents divorced. She was raised in many places all over the United States (about 16 places including Virginia, Atlanta, Miami) but mostly Florida. Her mother speaks only Portuguese, while her Dad can speak both Portuguese and English.

She graduated at Tisch School of the Arts(2016), New York University. She has a tattoo on her right ribs which reads "To build a home". She doesn't like to be stereotyped, she wants to try out varying roles. She has a sister named Kiara Moreno.

She went to the same school as actor Cole Sprouse at the same time but they did not know each other back then. Veronica likes partying: her pics of partying with her Riverdale co-stars went viral on net.

She has a fashion related Tumblr site which she prefers to keep private. She sees a therapist occasionally to keep herself free from the stress. She often participates in Comic Cons.

"Be specific when you set your goals. Visualize the career that you want for yourself."
